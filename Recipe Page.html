    Instructions

    Step 1: Prepare the Sugar Syrup In a saucepan, combine the sugar and water. Stir and bring it to a boil. Fry the balls in batches, stirring gently to ensure even cooking, until they turn golden brown. This may take about 7-8 minutes for each batch.
